About Obutonde Bwaffe

Obutonde Bwaffe (Our Environment) is Muteesa 1 Royal University's flagship environmental initiative, founded on the Buganda cultural principle of "obutonde bwensi" - the interconnectedness of all life and our responsibility to protect our natural heritage.


Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To cultivate environmental stewardship within the MRU community and beyond through education, action, and preservation of our natural resources, guided by indigenous knowledge and modern sustainability practices.


Vision

A future where the MRU community leads in environmental conservation, where sustainable practices are integrated into daily life, and where Uganda's natural beauty is preserved for generations to come.


Our Core Values

Environmental Stewardship

We believe in our responsibility to protect and preserve our natural environment for future generations.

Community Engagement

We work collaboratively with students and local communities to create meaningful change.

Education & Awareness

We prioritize environmental education as the foundation for sustainable change.

Cultural Preservation

We honor and integrate indigenous knowledge and Buganda cultural values in our work.

Our Story

Obutonde Bwaffe was established in 2025 by a group of passionate MRU students in the Faculty of Education, concerned about environment Conservation in Uganda. What will begin as a small campus tree-planting club will grow into a university-wide initiative with multiple projects and community partnerships.

Our name, "Obutonde Bwaffe," was carefully chosen to reflect both our Buganda heritage and our collective responsibility. It reminds us that the environment doesn't belong to us - we belong to it, and we must care for it as we would our own home.
